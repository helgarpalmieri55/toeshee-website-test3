What we deliver for fintech

KYC / AML Support

High-throughput identity verification and AML support for regulated fintech environments. Our teams manage verification queues, document review, sanctions screening, and EDD workflows at scale.

Payment Dispute Resolution

Structured handling of chargeback requests, failed payments, refund claims, and merchant disputes. We manage sensitive financial cases with speed and documentation discipline.

Account & Identity Management

Support for login issues, account recovery, 2FA problems, and user verification concerns. We resolve access friction while maintaining platform security protocols.

Regulatory Compliance Operations

Day-to-day operational support aligned with PCI-DSS, GDPR, MiCA, and regional AML directives. Audit-ready processes embedded into every workflow.

Fraud & Risk Escalation

Structured handling of fraud signals, suspicious transactions, and high-risk user cases. We route critical issues efficiently without creating compliance gaps.

Multi-Market Coverage

Multilingual and multi-jurisdiction support for fintechs expanding across markets. Our teams adapt to local regulatory expectations and platform-specific support flows.
